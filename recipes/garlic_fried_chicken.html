Garlic Fried Chicken

Description

Delicious garlic fried chicken that uses our best breading. All the crispiness of regular fried chicken with a favor blast of garlic to back it up.

Ingredients

  1. 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  2. 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  3. 1 teaspoon of salt
  4. 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  5. 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  6. 1/2 of whole milk
  7. 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
  8. 1 egg
  9. 1 cup of oil for frying

Directions

Step 1

In a shallow dish, mix together the farlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread, crumbs and flour. In a separate dish, whisk together the milk and egg.

Step 2

Heat the oil in an electric skillet and set to 350 degrees F (175 C). Dip the chicken into the egg and milk, then dredge in the dry ingredients until evenly coated.

Step 3

Fry chicken in the hot oil for about 5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Remove from the oil with a clotted spatula and serve.
