Garlic Fried Chicken

Description

Delicious garlic fried chicken that uses our best breading. All the crispiness of regular fried chicken with a favor blast of garlic to back it up.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 of whole milk
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of oil for frying
    Directions
  1. In a shallow dish, mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread, crumbs and flour. In a separate dish, whisk together the milk and egg.
  2. Heat the oil in an electric skillet and set to 350 degrees F (175 C). Dip the chicken into the egg and milk, then dredge in the dry ingredients until evenly coated.
  3. Fry chicken in the hot oil for about 5 minutes per side, or until the chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. Remove from the oil with a clotted spatula and serve.
