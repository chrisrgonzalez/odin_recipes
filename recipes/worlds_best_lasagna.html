World's Best Lasagna

Description

The most delicious lasagna in the world. Trust me there is nothing like this. From the fantastic cheese to the golden brown noodles. You will be craving more, more and more!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pounds of lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) can of tomato paste
  • 4 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound of mozzarella cheese slices
  • 3/4 cups of grates parmesan cheese
    Directions
  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons of parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon of salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer covered for about 1 1/2 hours while stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with an egg, the remaining 2 tablespoons of parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  6. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Cover with foil to prevent sticking and either spray the foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  9. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
